2. Information Collection
2.1 Personal Information:
We retain the authority to acquire various types of personal information, either directly or
indirectly identifying individuals, during your interactions with our Services. These categories

● Full Name: Your complete legal name.
● Physical Address: Your declared residential or business location.
● Date of Birth: Your birthdate, a pivotal personal identifier.
● Payment Information: Data concerning financial transactions and fiscal
engagements.
● Aadhaar Number: An essential element employed for Know Your Customer (KYC)
validation.
● Profile Information: Particulars submitted for user profile creation and administrative
management.
● Email Address: Your designated electronic correspondence.
● Social Media Information: Data shared through associated social media accounts.
● Voluntarily Provided Information: Supplementary information disclosed at your
discretion.

2.2 Non-Personal Information:
In addition to personal information, we gather non-personal data without inherent personal
identification. This category includes:
● Log Data: Information about your device and its interaction with our applications,
encompassing, but not limited to, IP addresses, device names, operating systems,
access times, and statistical data.
● Device Information: Details regarding the device used to access our Services, such
as IP addresses, geolocation data, unique device identifiers, browser types,
language preferences, and supplementary transactional details.
● GPS Data: Information pertinent to GPS routes and the operational status of GPS
devices during the use of our mobile applications.
● Traffic Data: Comprehensive data, inclusive of access times, access dates, session
identification numbers, access histories, and referring app addresses.

2.3 Information from External Sources:
At our discretion, we may supplement the data we collect from third-party sources or offline
interactions. This augmentation aims to enhance the quality of our Services and validate
user identities.
