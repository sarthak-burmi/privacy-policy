2. Information Collection

2.1 Personal Information:

We retain the authority to acquire various types of personal information, either directly or indirectly identifying individuals, during your interactions with our Services. These categories encompass:

  • Full Name: Your complete legal name.
  • Physical Address: Your declared residential or business location.
  • Date of Birth: Your birthdate, a pivotal personal identifier.

2.2 Non-Personal Information:

In addition to personal information, we gather non-personal data without inherent personal identification. This category includes:

  • Log Data: Information about your device and its interaction with our applications, encompassing, but not limited to, IP addresses, device names, operating systems, access times, and statistical data.

  • GPS Data: Information pertinent to GPS routes and the operational status of GPS devices during the use of our mobile applications.
  • Traffic Data: Comprehensive data, inclusive of access times, access dates, session identification numbers, access histories, and referring app addresses.

2.3 Information from External Sources:

At our discretion, we may supplement the data we collect from third-party sources or offline interactions. This augmentation aims to enhance the quality of our Services and validate user identities.
